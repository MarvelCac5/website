About me

A curious mind that loves to make things work.

A little about who I am and what drives me.

I'm Caleb, a rising junior at Queens High School for the Sciences at York College. I've always been the person who wants to know how something works — then take it apart, understand it, and put it back together better. That curiosity shows up everywhere from building PCs and repairing electronics to picking up speedcubing.

On the creative side, I design posters and infographics in the Adobe Creative Cloud, edit short- and long-form video, and have built an online presence through content creation on Twitch and YouTube. I'm also a black belt in karate, play soccer, badminton, and run track — I like staying disciplined and putting in the work.

Alongside the technical and creative work, I'm genuinely interested in business, branding, and social media — how ideas reach people and grow. I bring leadership, adaptability, and a focus on quality to every team I'm part of.

Outside of school, you'll find me on the soccer field or deep in a game — gaming is where a lot of my tech curiosity started, and soccer keeps me competitive, disciplined, and working with a team.
